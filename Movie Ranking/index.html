Top Movies of All Time According to Mark

My top 3 movies

The Lord of The Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The Fellowship of the Ring follows the journey of Frodo Baggins, a hobbit, who is entrusted with a powerful ring that must be destroyed to prevent the dark lord Sauron from conquering Middle-earth. Frodo is joined by a fellowship consisting of various creatures including humans, elves, dwarves, and hobbits, as they embark on a perilous quest to Mount Doom to destroy the ring. Along the way, they face numerous challenges and battles against Sauron's forces, testing their courage, loyalty, and determination to save their world from darkness.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

The Two Towers continues the epic journey of Frodo Baggins and his companions as they seek to destroy the One Ring. Meanwhile, the forces of good, including the remaining members of the Fellowship and the peoples of Middle-earth, confront the growing power of Sauron and his allies. Amidst battles and betrayals, alliances shift and heroes emerge, ultimately leading to the climactic showdown at the fortress of Helm's Deep and the edge of Mordor.

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

In The Return of the King, the final installment of The Lord of the Rings trilogy, Frodo and Sam continue their quest to destroy the One Ring, facing treacherous paths and the increasing influence of Sauron's evil. Meanwhile, the kingdoms of Middle-earth unite in a final battle against Sauron's forces, culminating in the epic confrontation at the Black Gate of Mordor and the ultimate fate of the One Ring. Sacrifices are made, heroes emerge, and the destiny of Middle-earth hangs in the balance as the story reaches its breathtaking conclusion.
